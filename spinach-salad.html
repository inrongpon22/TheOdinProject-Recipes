Strawberry Spinach Salad

Description

This strawberry spinach salad is a great way to get kids to eat spinach! Someone brought this salad to a potluck dinner, and I had to have the recipe. Since then, I've made it many times and been asked for the recipe every time I bring it somewhere.

Ingredients

Dressing:

  • 1/2 cup of white sugar
  • 1/2 cup of olive oil
  • 1/4 cup distilled white vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons of sesame seeds
  • 1 tablespoon of poppy seeds
  • 1 tablespoon of minced onion
  • 1/4 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon of Worcestershire sauce

Salad:

  • 1 quart of strawberries - cleaned, hulled and sliced
  • 10 ounces fresh spinach - rinsed, dried and torn into bite-size pieces
  • 1/4 cup of almonds, blanched and silvered

Steps

  1. Make dressing: Whisk together sugar, oil, vinegar, sesame seeds, poppy seeds, onion, paprika, and Worcestershire in a medium bowl. Cover and chill for 1 hour.
  2. Make salad: Combine strawberries, spinach, and almonds in a large bowl.
  3. Pour dressing over salad; toss to coat. Refrigerate for 10 to 15 minutes before serving.
